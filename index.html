About Us

We are a dynamic team participating in Smart India Hackathon 2024, driven by the passion for solving real-world problems through innovation and collaboration. Our project, Water Scheme System, is focused on addressing the challenges in drinking water supply scheme management by leveraging cutting-edge technology and creative thinking.

With expertise in software development, UI/UX design, and data analysis, our team is committed to delivering a solution that is not only functional but also impactful. We believe that through teamwork and determination, we can create a product that makes a meaningful difference.

Our mission is to revolutionize the way people interact with water management technology, enhance sustainability through innovation, and make complex processes more accessible and efficient. Throughout this hackathon, we are pushing boundaries and challenging ourselves to think outside the box.

What We Do?

Problem Statement

Development of a handheld/mobile-based O&M tool for asset and consumables inventories and finance management in the context of the drinking water supply scheme.

Our Solution

A comprehensive mobile app for recording, tracking, and managing water supply assets with features like GIS mapping, inventory management, and finance management with bill generation and UPI payment integration.

Team Collaboration

Working Together for Innovation

Our team thrives on collaboration, bringing together diverse skills and perspectives to tackle the challenges of water management. We believe that the best solutions emerge when we combine our expertise and work towards a common goal.

From brainstorming sessions to code reviews, every team member plays a crucial role in shaping our Water Scheme System. We encourage open communication, constructive feedback, and a shared commitment to excellence.

By leveraging each team member's strengths, we're able to create a comprehensive solution that addresses multiple aspects of water supply management, from technical implementation to user experience design.
